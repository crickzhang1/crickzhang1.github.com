Worldwide

Google Scholar

Science Citation Index (SCI)
  • Intellectual Property & Science: http://ip-science.thomsonreuters.com/
  • Check SCI journals: http://ip-science.thomsonreuters.com/mjl/
  • Search for SCI papers: http://webofknowledge.com/, under section Web of Science

SCI is the index system from ISI (Institute of Science Information of USA), which is divided into two categories: the 3500+ core SCI journals and the all 5600+ SCI Expanded (SCIE) journals includings the core ones.

Note: when searching for authors use full family name and abbreviation, e.g. Zhang C.

Engineering Index (EI)

http://www.engineeringvillage.com

Search for papers with EI usually occurs in the Eigneering Village site, and when check the database options: Compendex means papers are EIed, while Inspec not.

Note: when searching for authors use full names, e.g. Crick Zhang.
